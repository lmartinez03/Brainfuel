Common questions

An app I blocked still opens. Make sure the block group is enabled in the Blocked tab and that you granted the Screen Time permission. Brainfuel can only block apps once Screen Time access is allowed.

I passed the quiz but my app stayed locked. On the win screen, tap "Claim 15 minutes" to lift the block. Choosing "Take a break" keeps the app blocked on purpose.

Managing your subscription. Subscriptions are handled by Apple. To view, change, or cancel, open the Settings app, tap your name, then Subscriptions.
